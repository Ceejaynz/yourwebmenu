 FAQ

Can I change my menu?

We will set up your menu and the online system operation.  You can make changes to the menu as and when you need to .  You are able to make minor alterations at no charge (subject to reasonable use), however we will charge for a complete seasonal change.  

 Do I need to be a tech expert?

You will need to have either a computer or tablet that is connected to the internet for you to receive your orders.  That's all.  You will also need to make sure that someone is responsible for monitoring incoming orders.  For any complicated issues that arise which is highly unlikely, then you can contact us.

Why should I use your service?

Key differences from other online services:
* Minimalistic, extremely easy-to-use interface for your customers
* Mobile-friendliness using our super quick servers
* FLAT FEE  - Don't pay a commission on each and every sale
* Get Paid - Clients pay before the order is completed so you don't have to worry about no shows

Do I need to move my website to you?

NO!  This service operates independently of your website.  During the setup process, we will replicate the "look" of your website, if you have one, but the online ordering system will be hosted on our servers, completely seperately from your website.  You will need to contact your webmaster to update your website with a link to your online menu.

How long will it take to build?

This really is dependent on a couple of key things:  how complex your menu is and how quickly you can supply the content.  A text heavy menu with few photos for example will only take a couple of days.  We will be able to give you a turnaround estimate once we have all the materials to hand.

Is there a minimum contract period?

We offer a no contract service.  You will need to sign a Direct Debit for our service which is delivered on a month by month basis.  You can cancel at any time, however you must cancel at least 7 days before the end of your billing cycle or the service will continue for the next month and be billed accordingly.  There are no "pro-rata" rates offered.
